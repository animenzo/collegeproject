About Us

Mission

The Institute of Engineering and Technology's mission is to provide educational opportunities for aspiring students in order for them to inculcate the essential concepts as well as emerging breakthroughs in their chosen academic fields, and to impress the outlook and measures for cultural and social promotion, and to contribute to the overall framework of collective responsibility upon this path to growth and prosperity.

Goals :

  • To educate professionals capable of leading efforts towards innovation and the development of our country, and mankind in general.

  • To develop knowledge for the benefit of society via collaborative and individual efforts.

  • To instill an entrepreneurial spirit and the ability to design globally recognized solutions to societal and industrial challenges.

  • To collaborate closely with industry in pursuit of the aforementioned education and research aims, resulting in the creation of cutting-edge and economically viable innovations.

  • To create an environment that values talent and merit above everything else.

  • To integrate latest technology such as Information and Communication Technology with teaching, research, extension and governance.

  • To develop self reliant, enterprising and employable human resource.

  • To provide ambience in environment for freedom of expression in order to create liberal ethos.

RULES & INSTRUCTION

1. Admission a. Admission to B. Tech. First Year in 1st semester and Lateral Entry in B. Tech. Second Year in 3rd semester (for Diploma Holder and Science Graduate candidates only) will be made strictly as per the eligibility of the candidate.

b. Lateral Entry to Second Year Degree Course(s) in Engineering and Technology Course shall be permissible up to a maximum of 10% of the "Approved Intake" which shall be over and above, supernumerary to the "Approved Intake", plus the unfilled vacancies of the First year.

c. Any Foreign National who has obtained Diploma in a Foreign Institution (having an equivalency Certificate issued by the Association of Indian Universities) or Diploma in an Indian Institution shall also be eligible for Lateral Entry into the Second Year Degree Course(s). The Institutions having approval for the supernumerary seats in such Course(s) are only eligible to admit the Foreign Nationals as per the norms, else the Institution shall apply for the same on AICTE Web-Portal. However, the total Foreign Nationals admitted under supernumerary seats and the Lateral Entry shall not exceed the 15% of the "Approved Intake" in an Academic year.

2. Eligibility For Admission:

2.1 BASIC ELIGIBILITY

a. For B.Tech. First Year

i. Must Passed 10+2 or equivalent system from any recognized Central/State Board with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with Chemistry/Technical Vocational subject. The Aggregate Marks in Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry (or equivalent subjects) is securing minimum 45% marks (40% marks for SC & ST category candidates) without grace.

ii. Candidates who are appearing in the 10+2 or equivalent Examination may also apply.

B. For B. Tech. Second Year Through Lateral Entry

Candidates who have passed 3 or 4 year Diploma with minimum 60% marks from institutions recognized by AICTE or a State Board of Technical Education or B. Sc. degree (with Mathematics) recognized by any Indian Universities with minimum 60% marks are eligible for admission directly to Second Year (3rd Semester) in any branch of Engineering/Technology.

C) Direct Admission On Vacant Seats At Institution/College

The eligibility criteria for direct admission on seats remaining vacant in first year after entrance examination counselling shall be such as may be notified from time to time. The Academic Council shall have power to amend or repeal the eligibility criteria laid down at clause (a) & (b), as per the guidelines of All India Council for Technical Education (A.I.C.T.E.).

2.1.1 The Candidate seeking admission of B. Tech. Programme for both First Year and Second Year must have to selection basis of merit list of REAP.

(1) The number of seat available for admission through Lateral Entry Scheme in Second Year for any branch is the maximum 10% of the approved intake in addition to the number of vacant seat in the respective branch. The University uses REAP Process (50% seats of total intake) conducted by State Government rank for short listing candidate for its B. Tech. program.

(2) The number of seat available for admission through Lateral Entry Scheme and Transfer Cases in Second Year for any branch is the maximum 10% of the approved intake in addition to the number of vacant seat in the respective branch.

(3) Vacant Seats will be filled by Institute/College or University on the basis of merit list of percentage in 10+2 examination.
